Ushers and Greeters

Be an usher or a greeter. This is an important and vital service to our worshipping community. A greeter’s warm smile and a friendly “good morning” at the door makes anyone feel better. Our ushers make sure everyone finds a comfortable seat and has a worship bulletin. Ushers participate in the worship service too; lighting altar candles, collecting the offering and serving communion.

Contact Michelle Mills in the church office: michelle@cbcc-ucc.org

Audio Visual

Audio: Be a soundboard technician! Learn how to operate our soundboard, set up microphones and help our musical teams sound their very best.

Video: Be a Clicker! Weekly PowerPoint presentations need someone to “click” through the slides that project our hymns, liturgy and scripture.

Contact Coleman Todd at coleman@cbcc-ucc.org

Fellowship Hosts

Consider hosting fellowship one Sunday! Hosts don’t need to buy the food (they can) but our Sunday snacks are purchased in advance and already at the church. Fellowship hosts need to come in earlier on Sunday and set up. Hosts often leave worship a little early to be prepared for those ready for a good cup of coffee and a snack after worship and then tidy up thereafter. A great ministry for couples or two friends who love to entertain.

Contact Michelle Mills in the church office: michelle@cbcc-ucc.org

Choir

Choir Rehearsas are Wednesdays evenings at 7:00 PM and Sunday Mornings at 9:30AM in Sponsler Hall. Our church choir offers an inspiring anthem almost every week. We also have a talented rotation of soloists and ensemble teams that encompass other musical genres creating a diverse musical experience on Sundays. Come and share your musical talents with us!

HUNGER KNOWS NO SEASON . . . WE COLLECT FOOD EVERY WEEK!

CBCC helps support many food ministries In Cocoa Beach and throughout the Space Coast. We appreciate the food and other items brought in each Sunday and placed in our basket. Single serving items are used in ready to eat lunch bags available at the thrift store and at the church office. Other foods items are given to local food pantries.

UNDER THE OAKS. . .SECOND SATURDAY SUPPERS

Bring some friends and come enjoy a late afternoon of Food, Fellowship and often there is live music too. This is a great outreach opportunity. Crop Walk

Communities Responding to Overcome Poverty. CROP Walk is a fundraising activity that helps to feed hungry people in Central Brevard County as well as around the rest of the U.S. and world. 25% of funds raised stay local, used for Central Brevard Sharing Center’s Community Kitchen Program. See Church World Service link below to find out more about Crop Walk.

PASTORAL CARE

Spreading Love and Care

Caring members of our Church regularly visit members and friends in their homes, in hospitals, or in nursing homes. Birthday cards, Get-Well Wishes, and Miss-You cards are sent regularly. Those who receive it appreciate this personal contact provided by members of our church. The gift of giving & sharing is deeply rewarding.
