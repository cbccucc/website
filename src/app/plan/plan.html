Location

CBCC is located in the heart of downtown Cocoa Beach. Just one block south of Minuteman Causeway on the corner of S. 1st Street. You can park in the lot directly behind church or in lots on the north and south of the church on Atlantic Ave.

Worship

Sundays @ 10:00AM

Minister: Rev. Dr. Bill Tyson
Choir Director: Janet Johns
Organist: David Wrable
Education: Coleman Todd

All ages gather for worship at 10:00 AM in the church sanctuary. Our worship is primarily traditional worship with our church choir offering an inspiring anthem almost every week. We have a talented rotation of soloists and ensemble teams that encompass other musical genres creating a diverse musical experience on Sundays.

The first Sunday of each month is Holy Communion.

All are invited to the table

Adult Bible Discussions: 9:00 AM

After Worship is Fellowship

Meet & Greet, friends old and new!

Every week after worship plan to visit our fellowship time and get to know the friendly folk at CBCC.

Potluck Lunch! The first Sunday of every month we enjoy a terrific potluck lunch. Don’t worry if you didn’t bring a dish to share because there is always plenty to go around!

Fellowship is more than coffee! It is an important part of the gathered church life!
